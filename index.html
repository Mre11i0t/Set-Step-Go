How it works


Step

Install StepSetGo on your phone and start walking.

Earn

1000 steps = 1 SSG Coin 1000 OUTDOOR steps = 1.25 SSG Coins

Reward

Use your SSG Coins to get awesome rewards at the StepSetGo Bazaar.
